Amazon Affiliate Disclosure

Required FTC & Amazon Associates Disclosure

The Creative Well is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com. We only recommend products we genuinely believe in. Affiliate commissions never influence editorial decisions or the honesty of our content. Our recommendations reflect real knowledge and personal experience with the products and categories we cover.

Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate this site and deliver the newsletter. Specifically:

  • Email address and name — when you voluntarily subscribe to The Creative Well newsletter.
  • Usage data — standard analytics information such as pages visited, time on site, referring pages, and browser type. This data is aggregated and not personally identifiable.
  • Cookies — small files placed on your browser by this site and third-party services (including Amazon) to enable functionality, track affiliate referrals, and analyze traffic.

We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, or any other sensitive personal data. We do not sell your information to anyone, ever.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected on this site is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Sending the weekly Creative Well newsletter to subscribers who have opted in
  • Understanding which content is most useful to our readers through anonymous analytics
  • Tracking affiliate link performance to understand which product recommendations resonate
  • Improving site content, structure, and user experience over time

We will never use your email address to send unsolicited commercial messages unrelated to The Creative Well, and we will never share your email address with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Cookies & Tracking

This site uses cookies to function properly and to support our affiliate partnerships. Cookies used on this site include:

  • Analytics cookies — to understand how visitors use the site (e.g., Google Analytics). Data collected is anonymous.
  • Amazon affiliate cookies — when you click an Amazon link from this site, Amazon places a cookie in your browser that attributes any resulting purchase to our affiliate account. This cookie typically lasts 24 hours.
  • Functional cookies — small files that help the site remember your preferences during a session.

You may disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Note that doing so may affect site functionality and will prevent affiliate tracking — though this has no impact on your purchase price.

We do not use third-party marketing platforms to re-target you with ads based on your email subscription.
